The dangerous book of boys

The series follows the McKenna family as they cope with the death of their father, Patrick, a whimsical inventor. He leaves behind "The Dangerous Book for Boys," which inspires his youngest son, Wyatt, to have vivid fantasies where he reconnects with his father and learns life skills to navigate the real world.

Directed by Bryan Cranston and Greg Mottola,

Released: March 30, 2018.

The leading character is Wyatt McKenna, played by Gabriel Bateman.
