Annabelle

The movie "Annabelle" (2014) begins a year before the events of "The Conjuring." John Gordon gifts his pregnant wife, Mia, a vintage doll named Annabelle for her collection. Their happy life takes a terrifying turn one night when their home is invaded by members of a satanic cult. During the violent attack, blood from one of the cultists, Annabelle Higgins, drips onto the doll. Soon after, the couple experiences a series of disturbing supernatural events centered around the doll, realizing that a malevolent spirit has attached itself to it. They seek help from their priest, Father Perez, as the demonic presence grows stronger, threatening their newborn daughter.

Directed by John R. Leonetti.

Released:October 3, 2014.

The main characters in "Annabelle" (2014) include: Mia Form: Played by Annabelle Wallis, she is the pregnant wife who receives the Annabelle doll. John Form: Played by Ward Horton, he is Mia's husband and a medical student. Annabelle Higgins: A member of the satanic cult who attacks Mia and John, her blood falls on the doll. Father Perez: Played by Tony Amendola, he is the couple's priest who tries to help them. Evelyn: Played by Alfre Woodard, she is a neighbor and friend who offers support. It's important to note that the Annabelle doll itself is a central "character" in the movie, serving as a conduit for the demonic entity.
